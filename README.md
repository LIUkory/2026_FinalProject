The main concept of our project is to make a roguelike game by Unity. 
What we want to represent is a fast-paced, top-down roguelike that contains bullet-hell combat with satirical corporate economy. 
Players have to deal with the upcoming enemies and hire them after they are defeated. 
Fight for not only survival but also profit. 
Explore the randomly generated dungeon. 
And use money to gain more power and weapons which have lots of different features.
Our game is made with following features: Character & Skill, Lobby & UI, Money & Roguelike System, Boss design, Weapon & Gallery, Enemies, Shop NPC & Dynamic Bargaining System, Map design & Random Generation.
